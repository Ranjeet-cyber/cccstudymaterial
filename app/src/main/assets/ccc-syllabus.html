CCC Syllabus and Examination Schedule

  • NIELIT (National Institute of Electronics and Information Technology) conduct online examination for CCC (Course on Computer Concepts) exam. NIELIT conducts "CCC Monthly Exam Cycle".

    • The Duration of Examination is 90 minutes and you have to solve 50 objective type and 50 true false questions of one mark each. NO negative marking for wrong answer
    • Examination will be online and single terminal will be provided to every students.
    • From july 2014 NIELIT introduce CCC question paper in Hindi language.

  • CCC Syllabus
